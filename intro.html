What is Diablo 3?

  • Diablo 3 is a popular Action Role Playing game by Blizzard Entertainment, the makers of other popular games such as World of Warcraft, Overwatch, Hearthstone.
  • In Diablo 3, players take the role of one of the many classes available; such as a barbarian, witch doctor, or crusader to name a few, and fight the forces of evil led by Diablo himself.
  • Diablo 3 is a very popular game. It is often live-streamed by players to have audiences from around the world watch them play.
  • With an ability to have 4 players play online together at one time, cooperative games are very common in the Diablo 3 community.
  • Due to streaming, cooperative gameplay, and overall popularity, Diablo 3 gameplay and user's specific characters are often times discussed
  • There is a simple way to share information about Diablo 3 players experience and characters over the internet!
